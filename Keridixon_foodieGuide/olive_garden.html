Welcome to Olive Garden!

Olive Garden serves authentic Italian cuisine made from the freshest ingredients. The main reason I love going to Olive Garden is to have my salad with their homemade Italian dressing. But my ultimate favorite thing I always look forward to when I go there is their breadsticks with their homemade fettucine alfredo sauce that I love and cannot seem to find anything that even comes close it.
