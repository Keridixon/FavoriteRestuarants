Welcome to Freddy's Kitchen!

Freddy's Kitchen is an authentic Mexican restaurant. Every time I walk by it the food smells so good I instantly get hungry. The smells I get are enough to make one’s mouth water. I like Mexican food from time to time but here it's delicious and a place I like to treat myself to from time to time. I also love that on their windows they do paintings during different seasons of the year or even based on holidays. I think it’s a cool and fun idea.
